Channel Region

Component Index GroundhogHydroCRegion

Determine the submerged region of a channel given a quantity of water

Inputs

NameIDDescriptionType
ChannelCThe sectional curve profile of the channel; must be planar and vertically-aligned (i.e. it fills up in the Z-axis)Curve
AreaAThe desired area of the flow body. If unspecified it will try to guess a sensible value to use that can serve as a referenceNumber
PrecisionTThe number of units to be accurate to in finding a matching area. If unspecified it will use 0.01% of the area. Smaller values will take longer to calculate.Number

Outputs

NameIDDescriptionType
Channel(s)CThe perimeter(s) of the calculated water body or bodiesCurve
Area(s)AThe area of the calculated water body or bodiesNumber
