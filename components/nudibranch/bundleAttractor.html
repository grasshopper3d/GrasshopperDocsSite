Bundle Attractor

Component Index NudibranchAttractorsBundle

Generates an iteratively bundled pointlist for any given datatree population of points.

Inputs

NameIDDescriptionType
PointsPPoint Datatree to bundlePoint
RangeRBundling range Number
ForceFGlobal force for all the bundling pointsNumber
TensionTGlobal tension for all the bundling points within a tree branchNumber
Attraction/RepulsionAToggle between attraction and repulsionBoolean
FixedFiToggle between Fixed and NonFixed pointsBoolean
IterationsItNumber of iterationsInteger
RelaxStepsReIndividual relaxation steps in between iterations(smoother bundling, don't over do it)Integer
