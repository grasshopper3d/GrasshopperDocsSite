TweenerStartFromBrepAndCurves

Start a Tweener with a brep and two lists of curves. Produces an abstract "Tweener" object which can be previewed with a TweenerPreview component.

Inputs

NameIDDescriptionType
TweenRegionSurfaceSSurface of the tween regionBrep
AACurves to tween from. Need not be closed. Should be not too distant from the brep.Curve
BBCurves to tween to. Need not be closed. Should be not too distant from the brep.Curve
QualityQThe quality between 0 and 14Integer
