RoboDK

ADDON. Version 1.0.0.0. Released on 2019-02-05. Provides 3 components. Created by RoboDK Team. Features 9 video tutorials.
The RoboDK plugin for Rhino allows you to combine Rhino's CAD modeling software with RoboDK to program industrial robots. RoboDK simulation software allows you to get the most out of your industrial robot. Easily simulate and program industrial robots offline from your Rhino models and deploy robot programs on your robot with just a few clicks. RoboDK’s simulation and offline programming tools allow you to program robots outside the production environment, eliminating production downtime caused by shop floor programming.
