WombatGH

ADDON. Version 1.3.0. Released on 16-Jul-2019. Provides 59 components. Created by Andrew Heumann and Brian Ringley. Features 0 video tutorials.
Wombat for GH is a series of miscellaneous utilities aimed at improving modeling processes by streamlining common tasks. It includes components for operating on geometry, lists, files and folders, and visualizing the geometric properties of surfaces and meshes.
